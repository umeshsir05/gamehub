Help & Instructions

Chess

Click on a piece to select it, then click on a highlighted square to move.

Auto Player: Toggle "vs Computer" to play against AI. The computer will automatically make moves for the black pieces.

Tic-Tac-Toe

Click on any empty cell to place your mark (X). The first to get 3 in a row wins.

Auto Player: Toggle "vs Computer" to play against AI. Choose difficulty level for computer opponent.

Memory Match

Click on cards to flip them and find matching pairs.

Auto Player: Enable "Auto Play" to watch the computer solve the memory puzzle automatically.
